About Us

Bridging talent gaps across the Asia-Pacific region with deep industry expertise and AI-powered recruitment intelligence.

Our Story

Crossing Education was founded on a simple observation: the best talent and the best opportunities often exist in different countries, speaking different languages, operating in different regulatory environments — and neither side knows how to find the other.

We are a global recruitment consultancy specializing in finance and accounting placements worldwide. From day one, we have operated across borders — connecting companies and talent regardless of geography.

Our recruiters have placed candidates across Asia-Pacific, Europe, the Middle East, and the Americas. We understand local markets, local regulations, and local salary benchmarks — wherever "local" happens to be.
